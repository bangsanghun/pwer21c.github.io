t_obs : calcul(4)

\(s_p = \sqrt{\frac{(n_1-1)s_1^2 + (n_2-1)s_2^2}{n_1+n_2-2} }\)

On a \(\bar{x}_1=26,4111\), \(s_1=0078\), \(\bar{x}_2=26,5666\) et \(s_2=0,1658\)

\(s_p = \sqrt{\frac{(9-1)(0,078)^2 + (9-1)(0,1658)^2}{9+9-2}}=0,1296\)

\(t_{obs}=\frac{(\bar{x}_1-\bar{x}_2)-d_0}{s_p\sqrt{\frac{1}{n_1}+\frac{1}{n_2}}}=\frac{(26,4111-26,5666)-0}{0,1296\sqrt{\frac{1}{9}+\frac{1}{9}}}=-2,5452\)
